Motivations:

Having discussed for hours on end about the initial project we were undertaking we wanted to delve into a problem and come out with a solution rather than providing a new solution to the world. We believed that SafeMe was a necessary project to undertake to provide children with an easy and engaging means of information that helps them guide their way through internet services in a safe and secure manner. Educating them in a fun way whilst providing them with information that is super informative and understandable by their age group. Upon designing the site and going through the many sites that provided children based internet education we established that these sites were just illustrations and text and we wanted to provide more than just that. We aimed for colours, interactivity, drawings, quizzes and many more features to engage the audience, providing them a foundation for internet safety education. SafeMe is a site that enables all the to come together cohesively and it was our motivation of providing a free service to help the younger generations learn more about the unknown to them.

Aims:

Our aim at Multitech is to "Provide A Free Platform For Educating Younger Generations About Internet Safety". This is our main aim we established right from the beginning of starting this project as it was part of our passion to ensure we develop something that not just us could improve our web development skills on but to also help individuals become smarter and safer users of the web.

Goal 1: Provide A Free Platform

Free in today's world is hazy as many sites claim to be free but require you to login to access content but then it is limited due to needing a subscription to access this content. When it comes to education we believe it should be free and on our site we have a goal to make all facets of it free to every user without any ads or subscription based services. All the content is fully accessible and readily available to learn from.

Goal 2: Engaging Content

Establishing a site for a younger audience is both fun but challenging. This is due to the demographic of our audience requiring more visuals and interactive content for them to learn in various ways. We wanted to provide various ways for any individual to learn to account for all learning styles with such implementations of quizzes, clickable dilemma games, certificates, videos and images for greater depth. This enables the user to constantly shift their focus between activities and not have to read through hundreds of words just to learn a topic. More engaging interactive content is vital for us to achieve in the success of this project.

Goal 3: Extended Features

Beyond the scope of our minium viable features we had various amounts of features we would love to implement in the future to provide even more engaging platforms and tasks. Due to the timeline we are keeping within this goal is potentially forseeable in the near future and with an ever so growing audience continuous updates to this project could be made to extend the site.

Scope:

The scope of our solution was based on the 5 fundamental feautures we planned to implement into the SafeMe site being Modules, Achievement system, Quizzes, Annoymous messaging box and popup interactive facts. Upon other details in relation to the design of the site to reflect the demographic our project being children we also needed to implement various images, videos, colours and illustrations to enhance the sites interactability and engagement. The outcome of this project is to educate an individual with the means to go about into the internet and search freely without any issues as they will have been educated on what is safe, how to protect their accounts, malicious content and more which through these topics will provide them with the fundamental knowledge to be smarter and safer users of the web.
